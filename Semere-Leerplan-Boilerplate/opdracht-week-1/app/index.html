Zaha Hadid

The first woman to receive the Pritzker Architecture Prize

The picture depict the image of the Hoxton square, London, England

List of architectural buildings

Her international reputation was greatly enhanced in 1988 when she was chosen to show her drawings and paintings as one of seven architects chosen to
participate in the exhibition "Deconstructivism in Architecture" curated by Philip Johnson and Mark Wigley at New York's Museum of Modern Art.

  • 1991 - 1993 - Vitra Fire Station in Weil am Rhein, Germany
  • 1999 - 2002 - Bergisel Ski Jump, Innsbruck, Austria
  • 1997 – 2000 - Contemporary Arts Center, United States of America.
  • 2000 – 2005 - Phaeno Science Center, Germany.
  • 2001 – 2005 - Ordrupgaard Museum extension, Denmark.
  • 2001 – 2005 - BMW Administration Building, Germany.
  • 2005 – 2008 - Bridge Pavilion in Zaragoza, Spain.
  • 1998 – 2010 - National Museum of Arts of the 21st Century (MAXXI), Rome, Italy.
  • 2003 – 2010 - Guangzhou Opera House, Guangzhou, China.
  • 2007 - 2012 - Broad Art Museum, Michigan State University, East Lansing, Michigan, USA.
  • 2007 - 2013 - Dongdaemun Design Plaza, Seoul, South Korea.
  • 2009 - 2014 - The Wangjing SOHO office complex in Beijing, China.
  • 2016 - Port Authority Building (Havenhuis) in Antwerp, Belgium.
